# Stakeholder Map — MathQuest

**Version:** 1.0.0

---

## Stakeholder Matrix

| Stakeholder | Role | Interest | Influence | Engagement Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Students | Primary end users | Learning outcomes, engagement, fairness | Low (individual) | Product experience, feedback surveys |
| Teachers | Primary professional users | Time savings, student insight, curriculum alignment | High | Teacher advisory group, onboarding support, feature co-design |
| School Administrators | Institutional decision makers | Compliance, performance reporting, cost | High | Executive demos, ROI reporting, compliance documentation |
| District Coordinators | Multi-school oversight | Standardization, district-wide reporting | Very High | District pilot program, custom reporting |
| Parents | Indirect stakeholders (minors) | Child safety, learning progress | Medium | Privacy policy, parental consent flow, future parent portal |
| Platform Operators (Internal) | System management | Reliability, content quality, AI oversight | High | Internal tooling, runbooks, monitoring dashboards |
| Engineering Team | Builders | Technical clarity, feasibility, quality | High | Architecture docs, engineering workflow, definition of done |
| Product Team | Owners | Outcome delivery, roadmap execution | Very High | PRD, roadmap, success metrics |
| Legal/Compliance | Risk management | Privacy, regulatory compliance | High | Privacy policy review, compliance architecture sign-off |
| AI Provider | Technology partner | API usage, contract compliance | Medium | Provider SLA, usage monitoring, fallback design |
| Investors/Board | Financial oversight | Growth, retention, revenue | Very High | Executive summary, roadmap, business metrics |

---

## Communication Plan

| Stakeholder Group | Update Frequency | Format | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Teachers | Weekly (in-app) | Release notes, feature announcements | Product |
| School Administrators | Monthly | Performance report, platform updates | Customer Success |
| District Coordinators | Quarterly | Executive summary, compliance report | Business Development |
| Engineering Team | Sprint cadence | Sprint review, architecture decisions | Engineering Lead |
| Legal/Compliance | Milestone-based | Compliance review sessions | Product + Legal |
| Investors/Board | Quarterly | Business metrics dashboard | CEO/Product |
